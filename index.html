iCal Filter Configurator

Step 1: Save Your iCal Link

Paste your full iCal link below. Clicking the button will redirect you to GitHub to save a temporary file. This will trigger a background process to find all the event types in your calendar.

Step 2: Choose Events to HIDE

Your event types have been found! Check the box next to any event you want to remove. When you're done, click the button to save your final configuration.
